While Blackout was in its early phases, our team created visuals to express possible ideas for gameplay, the game logo and our company brand.

Blackoutâ€™s art direction was inspired by the movie series Insidious, where a main character was able to contact souls stuck in limbo. Dark tones and the house layout provided good inspiration for our game.

For the game story, we all liked the idea of creating a horror-themed, text-based adventure. Royelle Williams thought of the initial idea to collect candles to light up a dark-filled space. Zachary Sabra finalized the idea of the character is dead and stuck in limbo. The details were ironed out through a series of video chats with multiple team members. Nicholas Limner, Kenneth Morrison and Zachary Sabra wrote the script to provide a clear framework for the story.
